Our Story

Two Women, One Dream.

Lishan is the journey of two women. Our goal is not just to design a garment; but to gift every woman a "moment" that makes her feel special and unique.

There is no place for mass production in our designs. Each piece is handmade, and no two items are exactly the same. Just as every woman is unique, Lishan products possess their own distinct character.

In our beachwear, we bring together the comfort of summer with the elegance of high fashion and the grace of hand craftsmanship.

In Store Only: Marmaris

You can only experience our products in our boutique atelier in Marmaris. We do not have online sales; because every product's story begins with a touch.
